Welcome to the first post on the new site. It's a **terminal**, sort of. You
navigate it with your mouse or entirely from the keyboard, there are no commands
to memorize, and you can repaint the whole thing with a single keystroke.

## Why a TUI?

I spend most of my day in a shell, and there's a certain calm to monospace text,
tight contrast, and a blinking cursor. I wanted the site to feel like _home_.

> The best interface is the one that gets out of your way.

## What's under the hood

- **Next.js** with the App Router for routing and rendering
- **MDX** so posts like this one are just markdown files
- **CSS variables** for instant, flash-free theme switching

Here's the entire theme model, more or less:

```css
:root[data-theme="dracula"] {
  --bg: #282a36;
  --fg: #f8f8f2;
  --accent: #bd93f9;
}
```

Everything else — borders, muted text, subtle panels — is derived from those
three colors with `color-mix()`.

## Try it

Press <kbd>t</kbd> to open the theme switcher, or <kbd>?</kbd> to see every
shortcut. Then head back to the [blog index](/blog) or poke around the
[projects](/projects).
